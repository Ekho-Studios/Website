Midnight Dusk

Midnight Dusk is a roguelite shooter set in a dystopian cyberpunk setting, where megacorps war for global dominance. Shoot, stab, and explode your way through armed compounds in this 2D game inspired by games like Soul Knight, Destiny 2 and Enter the Gungeon. Craft guns and armor, hack your body with cybernetics; even flesh is mutable in Midnight Dusk. Test your tactical prowess in fast-paced firefights. So what are you waiting for? Grab your weapon, don your armor; it's time to fight.
